Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 20, 2026

Harvest Renovation respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect information you provide through this website.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect personal information that you voluntarily provide when you contact us or request an estimate through our website. This may include:

  • First name and last name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Project type
  • Preferred contact method
  • Property street address, city, and ZIP code
  • Budget range and timeline for larger projects
  • How you heard about us
  • Project details and any other information you choose to submit

2. How We Use Your Information

We may use the information we collect to:

  • Respond to estimate requests and general inquiries
  • Communicate with you about your project
  • Review project scope, timeline, and service-area fit
  • Improve our website and customer experience
  • Maintain internal business records
  • Follow up regarding requested services

3. Form Submissions

When you submit a request through our website, the information you provide may be processed by FormSubmit, a third-party form handling service, and forwarded to our business email for follow-up. This may include contact details, project information, and any other information you choose to include in your message. Please do not submit highly sensitive personal information through the website form.

4. Automatically Collected Information

Like many websites, we may automatically collect limited technical information such as browser type, device type, IP address, pages visited, and general usage data. This information helps us understand website performance and improve the site experience.

5. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Our website may use basic cookies or similar technologies for site functionality, analytics, and performance monitoring. These technologies help us understand how visitors use the site and improve its usability.

6. Third-Party Links

Our website may include links to third-party platforms such as Google Maps, Facebook, Instagram, and other third-party services. If you click those links, you may be directed to websites or services that operate under their own privacy policies and practices.

7. Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information only as reasonably necessary to operate our website, process form submissions through service providers, respond to your requests, comply with legal obligations, or protect our business rights.

8. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to help protect the information you submit to us. However, no method of internet transmission or electronic storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

9. Your Choices

If you would like to update, correct, or request deletion of information you previously submitted, please contact us using the information provided below.

10. Children’s Privacy

This website is not intended for children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any updates will be posted on this page along with a revised effective date.
