Review Engine + Tracking

Operational playbook for reviews, follow-up, and lead tracking

This page is your implementation guide for getting more proof, following up faster, and knowing where your leads actually come from.

Ask for a review after every finished job.
Track every form submit, call click, and lead source.

Review Request System

Three simple templates to start using after each completed job

Text message

Hi {first_name}, thank you again for trusting Harvest Renovation with your project. If you are happy with the work, would you mind leaving us a quick review? Your feedback really helps local homeowners feel confident choosing us. [Insert review link]

Email

Subject: Thank you from Harvest Renovation. Body: We appreciate the opportunity to work on your home. If you have a minute, we would be grateful for a review here: [Insert review link]. Thank you for supporting a local family-owned business.

Final walkthrough ask

If you love how everything turned out, the best way to support us is with a quick Google review. I can text you the link right now so it is easy.

Lead Follow-up Scripts

Use these when a new estimate request comes in

Auto-confirmation text

Thanks for reaching out to Harvest Renovation. We received your estimate request and will follow up soon. If you want the fastest response, call us at (832) 944-0267.

Missed-call text

Hi {first_name}, this is Harvest Renovation. I just tried calling about your project request. When you have a minute, text or call us back at (832) 944-0267 and we can talk through next steps.

24-hour follow-up

Hi {first_name}, checking back on your remodeling project. If you still want to schedule an estimate, reply here or call us at (832) 944-0267. Happy to help.

UTM structure

When you run Google Ads, Facebook, or email campaigns, use UTMs consistently so you can identify where each lead came from.

utm_source=google | utm_medium=cpc | utm_campaign=houston_kitchen_remodel
utm_source=facebook | utm_medium=paid_social | utm_campaign=retargeting
utm_source=google_business_profile | utm_medium=organic | utm_campaign=website_click
Minimum dashboard: Leads by source, leads by service page, calls by page, estimate-booked rate, win rate, and average job value.
